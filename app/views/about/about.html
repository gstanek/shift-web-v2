The Problem

As an hourly employee, you will always need to adjust your shifts. There are times when obligations outside of work come up and you need to find a replacement for a shift you have been scheduled for. There are also times when you need some extra cash and are looking for more shifts to pick up.

The process for an employee to make a change for a given shift is tedious. Usually, the process involves cycling through a list of phone numbers, calling each one until a co-worker agrees to take a given shift. Email is also used. In other instances, a list of available shifts is maintained via paper and pencil at the business. None of these methods are efficient or easily match shifts with the employees that want them most.

The Solution

Shift is an application that makes the process of posting shifts and picking up shifts easy for the employee. No longer will an employee need to go down a list of phone numbers and ask for a co-worker to take a shift they were assigned. On the other hand, if you need some extra cash, it's easy to see the shifts that are available when employees use Shift.
